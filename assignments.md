# Animal Conservation Projects

Research and projects focused on protecting endangered species and their habitats.

## Project 1: Save the Tigers

**Course:** Wildlife Conservation  
**Focus:** Bengal Tiger Conservation  
**Status:** Completed  

**Research Findings:**
- Only about 3,900 tigers remain in the wild
- Habitat loss and poaching are the biggest threats
- Conservation efforts in India have been successful

**What I Did:**
- Researched tiger populations and threats
- Created an awareness poster
- Presented findings to the class

**Impact:** Tigers are a keystone species - protecting them helps maintain ecosystem balance.

## Endangered Species Spotlight

### 🐆 Amur Leopard
**Population:** Fewer than 100 in the wild  
**Threats:** Habitat loss, poaching  
**Conservation:** Protected reserves in Russia and China

### 🐘 African Forest Elephant
**Population:** About 100,000 remaining  
**Threats:** Poaching for ivory, habitat destruction  
**Conservation:** Anti-poaching patrols, habitat protection

### 🐢 Hawksbill Sea Turtle
**Population:** Critically endangered  
**Threats:** Habitat loss, plastic pollution, hunting  
**Conservation:** Nesting beach protection, marine sanctuaries

## How You Can Help

1. **Reduce plastic use** - Marine animals suffer from plastic pollution
2. **Support conservation organizations** - Donate to wildlife protection
3. **Choose sustainable products** - Avoid items made from endangered species
4. **Educate others** - Share what you've learned about conservation
5. **Reduce carbon footprint** - Climate change affects all wildlife
