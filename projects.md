# Animal Categories

Explore different types of animals and learn about their unique characteristics, habitats, and behaviors.

## 🐘 Mammals

Warm-blooded animals that have fur or hair and produce milk for their young.

**Examples:**
- **Elephants** - Largest land animals with incredible memory
- **Dolphins** - Intelligent marine mammals that use echolocation
- **Kangaroos** - Marsupials that carry their young in pouches
- **Bats** - The only mammals that can truly fly

**Fun Fact:** Did you know that elephants can recognize themselves in mirrors, showing self-awareness?

## 🐦 Birds

Warm-blooded vertebrates with feathers, wings, and beaks. Most can fly.

**Examples:**
- **Bald Eagles** - Symbols of freedom with incredible eyesight
- **Penguins** - Flightless birds that "fly" through water
- **Ostriches** - Largest birds that can't fly but run very fast
- **Hummingbirds** - Tiny birds with rapid wing beats

**Fun Fact:** The smallest bird is the bee hummingbird, weighing less than a penny!

**Quick Bird Facts:**
- 🦅 Eagles can see 8 times better than humans
- 🐧 Penguins can jump 6 feet out of water
- 🦉 Owls can rotate their heads 270 degrees
- 🦜 Parrots can live over 80 years

## 🐍 Reptiles

Cold-blooded animals with scales or shells. They lay eggs on land.

**Examples:**
- **Snakes** - Legless reptiles that use their tongues to smell
- **Turtles** - Ancient reptiles with protective shells
- **Lizards** - Diverse group including chameleons and iguanas
- **Crocodiles** - Large reptiles that can live over 100 years

**Fun Fact:** Some turtles can live for more than 150 years!

**Quick Reptile Facts:**
- 🐍 Snakes can "smell" with their tongues
- 🐊 Crocodiles have the strongest bite force
- 🦎 Chameleons can change color in seconds
- 🐢 Sea turtles can hold their breath for 7 hours

## 🐠 Fish

Cold-blooded aquatic animals with gills for breathing underwater.

**Examples:**
- **Clownfish** - Brightly colored fish that live in sea anemones
- **Great White Sharks** - Apex predators of the ocean
- **Angelfish** - Beautiful freshwater and saltwater species
- **Goldfish** - Popular pets that can live up to 30 years

**Fun Fact:** The largest fish is the whale shark, which can grow longer than a school bus!

**Quick Fish Facts:**
- 🐠 Clownfish live in sea anemones for protection
- 🦈 Sharks have been around for 400 million years
- 🐟 Goldfish can live up to 30 years
- 🐠 Angelfish can recognize their owners

## 🐛 Insects & Arachnids

The most diverse group of animals on Earth.

**Examples:**
- **Butterflies** - Beautiful insects that undergo metamorphosis
- **Spiders** - Arachnids with eight legs and silk-producing abilities
- **Bees** - Important pollinators that live in complex social colonies
- **Ants** - Highly organized insects with complex societies

**Fun Fact:** There are more species of beetles than any other type of animal!

**Quick Insect Facts:**
- 🐝 Bees communicate by dancing
- 🕷️ Spiders can make silk stronger than steel
- 🦋 Butterflies taste with their feet
- 🐜 Ants can carry 50 times their weight
- 🦗 Crickets hear through their knees
