# Animal Learning Resources

Helpful websites, videos, books, and tools for learning about animals and conservation.

## Educational Websites

### Animal Information
- [National Geographic Animals](https://www.nationalgeographic.com/animals/) - Photos, videos, and facts about wildlife
- [World Wildlife Fund](https://www.worldwildlife.org/species) - Species information and conservation status
- [San Diego Zoo](https://animals.sandiegozoo.org/) - Detailed animal profiles and habitats
- [Smithsonian National Zoo](https://nationalzoo.si.edu/) - Panda cams and animal facts

### Conservation Organizations
- [WWF (World Wildlife Fund)](https://www.worldwildlife.org/) - Global conservation efforts
- [IUCN Red List](https://www.iucnredlist.org/) - Endangered species database
- [Defenders of Wildlife](https://defenders.org/) - Wildlife protection advocacy

## Educational Videos & Documentaries

### YouTube Channels
- [National Geographic](https://www.youtube.com/user/NationalGeographic) - Wildlife documentaries
- [BBC Earth](https://www.youtube.com/user/BBCEarth) - Amazing animal footage
- [Animal Planet](https://www.youtube.com/user/animalplanet) - Animal behavior and conservation
- [PBS Nature](https://www.youtube.com/user/pbsnature) - In-depth nature documentaries

### Popular Documentaries
- **Planet Earth** series by BBC - Stunning wildlife cinematography
- **Our Planet** on Netflix - Climate change and conservation
- **The Hunt** - Predator-prey relationships
- **Blue Planet II** - Ocean life and marine conservation

## Books for Young Learners

### Picture Books
- **The One and Only Ivan** by Katherine Applegate - Gorilla conservation story
- **Charlotte's Web** by E.B. White - Farm animals and friendship
- **The Wild Robot** by Peter Brown - Robot learns about wildlife

### Educational Books
- **National Geographic Kids Animal Encyclopedia** - Facts about hundreds of animals
- **The Hidden Life of Trees** by Peter Wohlleben - Forest ecosystems
- **March of the Penguins** - True story of emperor penguins

## Interactive Learning

### Games & Apps
- [Endangered Species Game](https://www.endangered.org/game/) - Learn about conservation
- [National Geographic Kids](https://kids.nationalgeographic.com/) - Games and activities
- [iNaturalist](https://www.inaturalist.org/) - Citizen science app for identifying wildlife

### Virtual Experiences
- [Google Expeditions](https://edu.google.com/products/expeditions/) - Virtual reality animal adventures
- [Zoo cams** - Live animal webcams from zoos worldwide
- [Ocean exploration** - Virtual submarine tours

## Field Guides & Reference

- **Peterson Field Guides** - Regional wildlife identification
- **Audubon Society Guides** - Bird watching resources
- **National Wildlife Federation** - Local wildlife information
